# Computer Science Concepts

Understanding a specific programming language is powerful, but understanding the underlying concepts of computer science gives you the keys to the kingdom. This section covers language-agnostic topics that form the bedrock of all computing.

---

## Why Study Concepts?

Languages come and go, frameworks rise and fall, but the fundamental principles of how computers work remain largely the same. Understanding binary, memory management, algorithms, and data structures allows you to:

-   **Write Efficient Code:** Know *why* one approach is faster than another.
-   **Debug Effectively:** Understand what's happening under the hood.
-   **Learn Faster:** Recognize patterns that repeat across different technologies.

## Topics Covered

This section will explore:

1.  **Machine Interaction:** How software talks to hardware.
2.  **Number Systems:** The math that powers digital logic.
3.  **Algorithms:** The recipes for solving computational problems.
